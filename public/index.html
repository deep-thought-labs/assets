Project 42 · Infinite Drive Ecosystem

Consider this your somewhat improbable catalog — the static files, chain data, and assorted cosmic detritus you’ll need to point your applications at precisely the right (or at least adequately interesting) corner of the universe. Everything here is public, perfectly linkable, and — in a rare display of universal generosity — there for the taking.

Fully open source. The whole thing is auditable by anyone with a browser and a healthy dose of curiosity. Feel free to use it however you see fit. We believe, rather strongly, that how you connect to the network ought to be something you can read, question, tinker with, and ultimately call your own — no black boxes, no nasty surprises lurking in the void. The code sits here in the open because it’s the only way we’d feel comfortable hitching a lift ourselves.
